The Two SHS Tracks

Explore the SHS Tracks

Under the Strengthened SHS Program, there are two main tracks: Academic and TechPro. Click any track to explore the clusters inside it and see where each one can take you.

Based on the official DepEd Strengthened SHS Program. All tracks, clusters, and subjects listed here follow the curriculum released by the Department of Education (DepEd) for the Strengthened Senior High School (SSHS) Program.
Simple Steps

How does this work?

Three easy steps and you'll have a much clearer picture of where you're headed in Senior High School.

01

Pick the track that sounds like you

Do you see yourself in college someday, or would you rather learn real-world skills and maybe earn a certification? That's basically the difference between the Academic and TechPro tracks. Start there.

02

Dig into the clusters inside that track

Each track has smaller clusters — like STEM, Business, Arts, ICT, and more. Click one and you'll see exactly what subjects you'll take, what skills you'll develop, and what it's designed to lead to.

03

Follow your navigation path

Every cluster shows you a step-by-step path: from your Grade 11 subjects, to the skills you'll build, to college courses or certifications, all the way to possible careers. That way you can make a real, informed choice — not just a guess.

About This System

Why we built ProjectTrackFind

Choosing a cluster is one of the biggest decisions you'll make in Junior High School. We built this to make sure you actually understand what you're choosing — not just go along with what your friends pick.

Made for Grade 10 students specifically

Not for everyone in general — for you, right now, as you prepare to move into Senior High School and actually have to make this choice.

Uses the real DepEd curriculum

Everything here is based on the official Strengthened SHS Program from DepEd. The subjects, clusters, and tracks are not made up — they're the real thing.

One place for all the info you need

Instead of Googling a hundred different things and still feeling lost, everything about each cluster is organized in one clear, easy-to-read place.

Part of a real research study

This is a system built and studied by Grade 12 researchers at STI College Cubao to measure whether a digital navigation tool actually helps students like you make better cluster choices.
